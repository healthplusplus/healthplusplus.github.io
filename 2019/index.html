About

health++ is a two-day event that will bring together 300 engineers, designers, business experts, and healthcare professionals for a weekend of ideating, designing, and building for affordability in healthcare both domestically and globally.

Discover Needs
Find a need you’re passionate about. Discover needs during the problem pitching session, or inspire others and pitch your own need.
Form Teams
Healthcare is interdisciplinary, so assemble an interdisciplinary team, complete with many talents.
Build
Create innovative prototypes and business models. Hacking isn’t purely technical.
Showcase
Present your work to an esteemed panel of judges for the opportunity to kickstart your idea and share your project on the big stage.
